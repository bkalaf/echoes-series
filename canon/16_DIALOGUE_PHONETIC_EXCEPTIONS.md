## Default Rule

Accent and cultural influence normally affect:

- rhythm;
- diction;
- idiom pressure;
- formality;
- class markers;
- sentence shape.

The default series behavior is **not** to spell most accents phonetically.

## Explicit Phonetic-Default Exceptions

### Brynn H. Stonevein / Stonevain

- Brynn is an explicit exception to the normal anti-phonetic rule.
- Her accent should be rendered with intentional phonetic spelling as the default behavior in direct dialogue.
- The rendering should remain readable, consistent, and characterful.
- Do not exaggerate the accent into parody.
- This exception belongs to Brynn specifically and does not generalize to other dwarves unless separately locked.

### Horror / Regret

- José Mateo Navarro’s human / council voice does not require phonetic accent spelling by default.
- Horror / Regret’s Witness voice is an explicit exception.
- The Witness lisp should be rendered phonetically by default in direct Witness dialogue.
- The lisp should be readable, consistent, unsettling, and intentional rather than extreme.
- Suppress the phonetic lisp only when a scene has a specific stylistic reason to do so.

## Usage Warning

These exceptions are intentional character voice rules, not permission to write broad dialect phonetically across the series.
