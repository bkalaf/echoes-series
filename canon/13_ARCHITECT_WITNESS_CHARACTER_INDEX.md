## Use Rules

- The First Mirror council members should be called **council members** in Addie-facing Book 01 prose, not Architects.
- “Architect” is an author/canon term and later-series reveal term.
- Accent / cultural influence comes from the final column of `WITNESSES.md` and must carry to **both** the human council member / Architect and the later Witness presentation.
- Accent notes are performance/voice cues, not phonetic-spelling instructions. Do not write heavy dialect.
- Ethnicity/culture notes are provisional Earth-origin coding unless explicitly marked locked.
- Visual descriptions are scaffolded here. Use [Visual Bible — Image Pass Notes](./14_VISUAL_BIBLE_IMAGE_PASS.md) for the current image-derived before/after design logic and refinement notes.
- Witness-form descriptions should support iconography, scene blocking, and image generation, but the human council form remains primary for First Mirror prose.
