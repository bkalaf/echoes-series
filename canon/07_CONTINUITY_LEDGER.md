# Continuity Ledger

This file tracks continuity risks, unresolved questions, and items that should not be accidentally overwritten.

## Active Hard Locks

- Series: **Echoes of Eidolon**.
- Series structure: 18 books, 6 trilogies.
- Tagline: **Control. Alter. Deceit.**
- Primary factions: Concord / Ruin / Schism.
- Current world model: one nation, many city-states / civic regions.
- Supercontinent: Aurreth.
- Mother AI: Dianne.
- Mother is fallible and limited.
- Mother’s long-term arc includes discovering Eidolon is reciprocal: she shapes the world, but the world also teaches and reshapes her.
- Addie is from **Qasr Siroth**. Early Skyrend Peak scenes are travel / work-assignment scenes, not her home-city baseline.
- High Sovereign: Donvaris Q. Drumpf.
- DJT coin acronym: Judax Tyrannis.
- One obelisk per city at exact center.
- Citadel = government landmark.
- Only visible ships: Michael, Raphael, Gabriel, Uriel.

## Chapter Drafting Locks

When rewriting an existing chapter:

- Do not remove previously established hard requirements.
- Do not remove section breaks unless explicitly requested.
- Do not reorder events in an L4 reconstruction outline.
- Do not swap who discovers information.
- Do not change who delivers a key line.
- Do not replace a specific emotional turn with a generic summary.
- Do not collapse a scene’s mechanics into exposition.
