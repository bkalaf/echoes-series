# Book 01 Calendar Timeline

This file locks the working date / weekday layer for the currently defined Book 01 chapters and visible subchapters.

## Calendar Rule

- Eidolon months have **29 days**.
- A Conjunction occupies one full month in narrative calendar terms.
- Day 1 of a Conjunction month is the **purple beacon** / 48-hour warning.
- Day 2 is the **blue beacon** / 24-hour warning.
- Day 3 is the **green beacon** / effective Conjunction onset.
- Book 01 opens on **Yearsend 29, Kindlemask**.
- The Book 01 Conjunction month begins with **Hearthwake**.
- **Sonntag is not an active weekday in Book 01.** It remains a hidden / future weekday until Book 18.

## Weekday Progression Used Here

Book 01 uses the active 8-day weekday cycle. Since Yearsend 29 is Kindlemask, the next day cycles back to Dravenkar.

| Date | Weekday | Beacon / Story Function |
|---|---|---|
| Yearsend 29 | Kindlemask | Prologue / Mother prepares Brickett before the public crisis begins. |
| Hearthwake 1 | Dravenkar | Purple beacon lights; crisis announcement / 48-hour warning. |
| Hearthwake 2 | Stonewake | Blue beacon lights; 24-hour warning / public extraction and Conclave day. |
| Hearthwake 3 | Aegalor | Green beacon lights; Conjunction begins / Day One operations. |

## Drafting Rule

When revising or expanding any Book 01 chapter, include the date and weekday in the chapter planning metadata. Do not force characters to state the date unless it serves the scene. The metadata layer should remain explicit even when prose keeps the calendar implicit.
