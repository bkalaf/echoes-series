## Placement Rationale

This interlude should come after the Stormspire / Restless Souls threshold chapter and immediately before the Horror challenge because it teaches the reader how to read the solution: the point is not simply killing the monster, but finding the path back out and understanding the knot that made the maze.

## Do Not Move Without Review
