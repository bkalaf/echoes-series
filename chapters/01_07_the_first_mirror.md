# Stub Structure

## 1. The Beginning of the String

Addie leaves the Conclave through the thread / seam transition.

Silence warns her that what she is about to see is real enough to matter, but not complete enough to trust as transcript.

The warning should include an apology for subterfuge.

## 2. The Table Under the Wrong Sky

Addie enters the wrong-sky council chamber.

She sees the ring, name-flames, one deliberate empty chair inside the circle, Noel outside the circle in amber, and Dianne present remotely through the weave / wall.

Do not have Addie call them Architects.

Do not have Addie count the seats loudly in narration unless absolutely necessary.

Do not have Addie think Noel’s seat belongs to her.

Correct Addie question:

> Why does the man outside the circle have authority over the circle?

## 3. Read the Room

Dangerous content pressure-blocks.

Addie cannot hear the full argument, so Silence directs her to watch reactions.

Addie notices:

- Hans slows the room;
- José is exhausted by human logistics;
- Seraphine is furious at hidden law;
- Alessandra is terrified of purity logic;
- Soren and Elowen are arguing for life beyond human survival;
- Gideon and Corvin disagree over practical secrecy and public truth;
- Wenliang treats objects as vessels of people, not replacements for them;
- Meera refuses body-as-parts thinking;
- Malcolm worries security will become fear;
- Ji-hoon is disturbed by models treated as verdicts;
- Kaelen rejects conquest disguised as restoration;
- Solomon’s face matches Ash.

## 4. The First Alignment

The twenty-four city-state clue becomes one of the clearest audible fragments.

Addie recognizes that this number is too exact to be coincidence with the structure of Erzengel / Eidolon.

Noel speaks after the values are stated. Addie may not hear his full argument, but she sees the room’s center of gravity shift toward him.

## 5. The First Dovren

Second Mirror begins.

This mirror is clearer because its reveal scope belongs to Book 1.

Show early Dovren / High Sovereign symbolic logic, coin logic, household cruelty, and the sentence:

> “That should survive, even if the people don’t.”

Keep the focus on Book 1 political-symbolic truth, not full ark truth.

## 6. The Riddle of Three Threads

Return to Witness space.

Horror / Regret names the three thread candidates:

- Red Thread of Fate;
- Ariadne’s Thread;
- Thread of the Norns.

Horror gives the riddle:

> “Bring the thread that makes the horned dead charge.”

## 7. The Van’kareth Gate

Addie returns to ordinary time.

Rhün has been waiting approximately two hours.

The blue beacon still burns; the green beacon is expected tomorrow night.

Rhün escorts Addie through Aquila Matara to Mae’vyri H. Van’kareth’s estate gate.

End on the sharpening sound / Mae household threshold, leading into Chapter 8.
