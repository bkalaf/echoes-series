This city has existed continuously for over 2,000 years.

Its appearance reflects two millennia of gradual expansion, repair, adaptation, reinforcement, and repurposing rather than demolition and complete reconstruction.

Buildings were enlarged, connected, fortified, bridged, and extended across countless generations.

Major civic buildings stand upon ancient foundations surrounded by centuries of later additions, annexes, workshops, homes, courtyards, walls, arcades, bridges, and supporting structures.

Favor accumulated medieval urban growth over master-planned symmetry.

Favor strong vertical layering and clear height variation.
