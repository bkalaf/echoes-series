## Core Scene

José climbs into the helicopter / flying machine and sees the city-killing wave approaching.

The ships appear to drive up the face of the wave or climb through impossible force. The companions cannot understand what they are seeing yet.

## Vision Function

This vision should deliver the scale of the catastrophe without showing the actual carnage.

It should also plant the later propulsion-system reveal:

- from the companions' angle, the ships look like they are riding up the wave;
- the deeper truth is that they are actually going airborne to avoid being smashed;
- do **not** explain that here.

This vision cuts before impact and shifts into `01_04 — The Cities That Disobeyed`.

# Beat-By-Beat Draft

## Beat 1 — José Enters The Helicopter

1. José climbs into the helicopter / flying machine.
2. He is still carrying the momentum of his last command decisions.
3. The ships are pulling away behind / below him.
4. The civilians remain on the pier.
5. The scene should feel like there is no time left for reconsideration.

## Beat 2 — He Sees The Wave

1. José looks toward the water.
2. He sees the wave.
3. Required line:

> "Oh Jesus Christ. Get us in the air now."

4. This should be the first moment when José's control breaks into open fear.
5. The companions see the wave too.
6. The wave is enormous, city-killing in implication.

## Beat 3 — The Pier Breaks Into Panic

1. People on the pier begin screaming.
2. They run.
3. They do not have anywhere meaningful to go.
4. Carrio or Brickett can produce the blunt holy-shit reaction.
5. The companions understand that the earlier ship choice has now become immediate death for the people left behind.
6. Do **not** show impact.

## Beat 4 — The Ships Climb

1. The ships go full speed.
2. They appear to drive up the face of the wave or climb through impossible force.
3. The visual should imply some kind of magic / impossible propulsion effect.
4. None of the companions understand the mechanism.
5. From their angle and the dusk conditions, the ships look as though they are riding the wave up.
6. The motion should look impossible.
7. The companions should not yet know whether they are watching escape, impact, or a miracle.

## Beat 5 — Hidden Truth For Later

1. The deeper truth is that the ships are actually going airborne to avoid being smashed.
2. Do **not** explicitly say this in the vision.
3. A later vision / Mother conversation can reveal that the emergency forced a full propulsion-system test and that it worked.
4. That later payoff should explain why no later full sails / full engines test is required.

## Beat 6 — Cut Before Carnage

1. Do not show the wave impact.
2. Cut on the impossible upward motion.
3. Let the screams remain behind them.
4. Shift into `01_04 — The Cities That Disobeyed`.
