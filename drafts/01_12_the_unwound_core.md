# Beat-By-Beat Skeleton

## Subchapter 1 — The Only Successful Group

1. Team Three returns to Mae’s Aquila Matara estate through the heavy obsidian doors.
2. The house is shielded from the green-pulsing chaos outside, but tension is already inside.
3. Max waits in the foyer, rigid and unhappy.
4. He says only one group was successful.
5. Addie asks what he means.
6. Max tells them to get inside because the others are not happy.
7. Max notices Brynn and struggles with her accent / presentation.
8. He mutters that they will do charades if they have to.

10. Move quickly toward the family room.

## Subchapter 2 — Green Light at the Window

1. Mae pauses by the window.
2. Aquila Matara is washed in unnatural green beacon light.
3. The capital looks beautiful and sick at the same time.
4. Addie registers that the city has entered a phase no one in living memory has seen.
5. The family room is already crowded with the other six companions.
6. The other teams look restless, embarrassed, and irritated by failure.
7. Awkward introductions to Brynn follow.
8. Brynn’s dialect creates friction, but she refuses to soften herself for noble convenience.
9. Rhün bolts or jerks toward the projection wall/fireplace feed, pulling attention to the broadcast.

## Subchapter 3 — Rogue Moderator

1. The wall-mounted projection / broadcast relay shows the hyena reporter and Australian correspondent.
2. The broadcast frames Addie as a rogue Chief Moderator who attacked innocent Schism citizens before the green beacon.
3. Dovren appears or is quoted, smooth and disappointed.
4. The smear campaign is immediate and politically coordinated.
5. Mae cuts the feed.
6. Mae curses openly and calls it a disinformation campaign.
7. Mae says Dovren is trying to destroy Addie’s reputation before Addie even has one.
8. Addie should feel the public-cost pressure: her first major uncontrolled power display has already been turned into a weapon.
9. Do not let the scene become a media-analysis lecture. Keep it personal, urgent, and useful.

## Subchapter 4 — The Man Who Lifted Me

1. Yurislav, in the shadows, begins running his silver coin across his knuckles.
2. He defends Dovren, not because he is politically stupid, but because Dovren once treated him as special.

10. Tallandra asks where they came from / why they match.
11. Yurislav says his has a strange symbol he never identified.
12. The restaurant group reacts together because they know what symbol is coming.

2. Tallandra gets up and removes her cloak clasp.
