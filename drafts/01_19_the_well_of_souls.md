- Beastfolk embody outward-expression traits; mythos embody hidden/internal traits, but human hidden traits do not cancel out cleanly.
- The 1/3 human, 1/3 beastfolk, 1/3 mythos pattern created immediate identity sorting and civil unrest.
- Wiped adults and children with no finite beginning could not psychologically process being “turned on” mid-story.
- Mother misread the difference between AI continuity and human narrative continuity.
- The Well of Souls is a storage / reincarnation / population-balance pressure system.
- The forty-eight later cities are not organic accidents; they were seeded as controlled relief valves connected to the beacon network.
- Dovren sees that he can weaponize the splits into factions and collaborators.
- Mother begins realizing she needs backup plans and worst-case planning.
- The Overseer/Mother identity remains hidden from the companions but is heavily implied.
