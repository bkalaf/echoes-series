## Core Function

This source interrupts Gregory’s avoidance pattern with a fake-world parable about paper peace. It teaches that an agreement can be morally beautiful on parchment and strategically catastrophic in consequence when it lets an aggressor treat delay as permission.

The lesson is not that negotiation is always cowardice. The danger is negotiation that mistakes appeasement for peace, hands someone smaller the first bill, and gives the bully a map of unenforced rules.
