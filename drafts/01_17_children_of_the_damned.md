## Title Rationale

The title should initially read like a dark pun on the Bountiful Dam. By the end, it becomes literal: the companions realize they are standing in a graveyard of calcified frogfolk eggs / young whose lives were taken because Dovren dammed the water and let generations die.

The title also becomes the suggested new name for the company Mae transfers away from her family at the end of the chapter.

1. show exhaustion after days of failed investigation;
2. let Addie find the actual logic of the dam through the word **cataclysm** and the frogfolk life cycle;
3. move the group to Pollyr Fen and establish the city as beautiful, wetland-integrated, and spiritually organized around water / young / nursery life;

This is a breakthrough chapter, but the breakthrough should hurt.
